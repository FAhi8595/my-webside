About Us

Founder’s Vision

The founder is believing in the power of community support. ICST actively engages with local communities, businesses, and philanthropic organizations to establish partnerships that further support our mission. To create a conducive learning environment, the founder prioritized the establishment of physical and infrastructure facilities with cutting-edge technology, architectural marvel and esthetic appeal of world-class learning environment.

Founder’s VISION

The founding vision of ICST is rooted in the belief that higher education should be a transformative force capable of breaking down socio-economic barriers. ICST was established with the explicit purpose of providing a job oriented higher education for those with limited financial means, creating an environment where every aspiring student, regardless of their economic background, can access quality education and embark on a journey of enfranchisement. The founder envisioned a university with inclusivity that ensure students from economically disadvantaged backgrounds have equal opportunities to pursue higher education.

The founder’s vision is a commitment to social responsibility too. ICST engages in initiatives that address societal challenges, contributing to the betterment of communities and emphasizing the role of higher education in creating positive social change, and contributing towards human resources development of our country.

Eng. M.H.A HIRAS

A warm welcome to ICST: a beacon of learning and innovation

It is with immense pleasure that I extend a warm welcome to every one of you as the Chairman of our newly built ICST University Park. With the guidance of our Founder at ICST, a remarkable journey began, marked by a shared dedication to shaping the future through higher education.

Our state-of-the-art campus represents more than just physical space; it embodies our mission to cultivate an environment that nurtures intellect, encourages creativity, and instills enduring values. Our vision is to establish a hub of academic distinction, innovation, and inclusivity. We are steadfast in our commitment to ensuring that ICST serves as a source of inspiration, offering unwavering support to every student in unlocking their full potential. Our focus will encompass not only education but also personality development through extracurricular activities. I sincerely extend an invitation to each of you to collaborate in shaping ICST into a hub where innovation flourishes, and where inclusivity and equitable opportunities for pursuing higher education are prioritized.

Sincerely

Message from the Professor

Jayantha Lal Ratnasekara

As the Vice Chancellor, I am delighted to witness the inauguration of ICST University Park and embark on a journey toward academic excellence and innovation.

ICST is committed to fostering an environment that nurtures intellectual growth. All the academic programs at ICST are crafted to prepare students not only for the challenges of today but also for the opportunities of tomorrow. All our academic programs are industry collaborated and job oriented.

Let us together build a legacy of academic excellence that resonates far beyond!
