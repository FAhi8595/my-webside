About ICST

ICST University Park, officially known as the International Campus of Science and Technology, was established to provide job-oriented higher education, particularly for those with limited financial means. The university emphasizes inclusivity, aiming to offer equal opportunities for students from diverse backgrounds. It is dedicated to transforming lives through accessible education and empowering students to pursue their academic aspirations, regardless of their economic background. The university also has a strong focus on student accommodation, with residences designed to blend comfort, practicality, and visual appeal.

  • Cutting-edge programs in Computing, Engineering, and Management
  • Modern labs and research facilities
  • Experienced and passionate faculty
  • Strong focus on leadership and innovation
  • Supportive and inclusive campus environment
