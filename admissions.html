Admissions

HDIT (Higher Diploma in Information Technology)

Our HDIT program is designed for students passionate about technology and innovation. Gain hands-on experience in software development, networking, and IT management.
Eligibility: G.C.E. A/L or equivalent.

Apply HDIT

BAIT (Bachelor of Arts in Information Technology)

The BAIT program prepares students for a career in IT with a strong foundation in programming, systems analysis, and project management.
Eligibility: G.C.E. A/L or recognized diploma.

Apply BAIT

ACCA (Association of Chartered Certified Accountants)

Our ACCA program is ideal for aspiring accountants and finance professionals. Learn global accounting standards and business skills.
Eligibility: G.C.E. A/L or equivalent in any stream.

Apply ACCA
